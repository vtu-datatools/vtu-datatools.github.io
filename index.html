About

Our working group formed to be a resource for tenants in their struggle against the increasing precariousness of the housing situation in Vancouver.

Whether you're an expert coder or you have an idea where to find some data, we want you to join our team! We organize on Discord.

Join Now!

Please donate

We are supported by the efforts of the VTU and all the wonderful work they do.
If you have the means we encourage that you become a monthly donor to help us in this work.
